Facebook Comment Analysis

Coming soon. Please check back at this page after Sunday, November 21, 2021.

This app organizes and analyzes social media comments copy-pasted from a Facebook post. The app first puts each comment in a row in a spreadsheet with a corresponding commenter, a unique comment ID, and the number of "likes". The app also creates an analysis tab, which includes a table of the most common two words in a row (with the frequency), the most common three words in a row (with the frequency), and the most "liked" comments.

For questions, comments, or suggestions for improvement on the apps, please email Sam Klump at sam.klump@outlook.com.
