    Census Analysis Apps

    The census analysis apps help with parsing and analyzing census data. The app converts poorly structured flat tables on data.census.gov into linkable tables in a spreadsheet with categorical data that sums to 100% within each category. The app then performs descriptive statistical analysis on the data by converting data into percentages, computing correlations, and deriving measures of centrality (mean, quartiles, etc.). Finally, the "Advanced Analysis" app pulls data directly from the census API and plots data to find correlations.


    Traffic Analysis Apps

    Traffic analysis apps include modules for parsing Alaska Department of Transportation traffic volume and speed data, converting IHSDM crash prediction model HTML outputs into Excel files, and trip assignment modeling with mapping capabilities.
