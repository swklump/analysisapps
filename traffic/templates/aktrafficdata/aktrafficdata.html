AKDOT Traffic Data Compiler App

This application compiles individual traffic data (volumes, speed) from the Alaska Department of Transportation & Public Facilities website into one combined file.

  • Step 1:
    • Download "Multi-Day Volume" or "Monthly Speed" files from alaskatrafficdata.drakewell.com. Do not edit the files after downloading. Send the files to a compressed zipped folder. If wanting to compile both volume and speed data, create two separate zipped folders.

  • Step 2:
    • Upload the zipped folder of "Multi-Day Volume" or "Monthly Speed" files.

    • Overview

    This application reorganizes traffic volume and speed data from the Alaska Department of Transportation and Public Facilities website to be more easily analyzed and combines multiple data files into one continuous table in Excel. The hourly volume and monthly speed files on the AKDOT&PF website come in a format that makes analysis difficult and can only be downloaded for one month at a time. This app changes the format to be a single table with row values for easy filtering and multiple months of data.
